INSTRUCTIONS FOR BADR_MELLAL
==================================================

QUALITY REQUIREMENTS:
• 5-10 high-quality photos
• Face occupies 50-70% of image
• Clear, well-lit photos
• Multiple angles and expressions
• Resolution: 640x640+ pixels
• Sharp, non-blurry images

PHOTO TYPES:
1. badr_mellal_front.jpg (front-facing)
2. badr_mellal_smile.jpg (smiling)
3. badr_mellal_left.jpg (slight left turn)
4. badr_mellal_right.jpg (slight right turn)
5. badr_mellal_up.jpg (slight upward look)

AVOID:
• Blurry images
• Sunglasses or masks
• Multiple faces in frame
• Very dark or bright lighting

After adding photos, run:
python setup.py --add-faces
